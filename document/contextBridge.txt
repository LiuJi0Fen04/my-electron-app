        ReadableStream, WritableStream

    contextBridge exists to safely expose only specific APIs from the Electron main or preload process to the untrusted renderer.

    Since MessagePort is a transferable object and not serializable in the traditional sense, it doesnâ€™t play well with structuredClone.
